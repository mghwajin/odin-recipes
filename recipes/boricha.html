Tteokbokki - Korean Spicy Rice Cakes



The original recipe can be found on My Korean Kitchen.

Prep time: ~10 min

Cook time: ~15 min

*Note: Some barley tea is available in teabags. These are a much quicker alternative to roasting the barley grains yourself!

Ingredients

  • 2 liters drinking water
  • 3 tablespoon roasted barely grains

Cooking Instructions

  1. To roast barley beans, first preheat a skillet over low heat.
  2. Add the barley and stir around until it turns a rich brown color (about 10 minutes).
  3. Set aside the barley in a tea strainer container for now (or a large tea bag!)
  4. Boil the 2 liters of water in a kettle or pot until it is actively boiling.
  5. Add the roasted barley to the water and lower heat to low (or medium-low).
  6. Heat for 5 more minutes, then remove the tea from heat. Remove the container of roasted barley.
  7. Let the tea cool until it can be transferred into a jug.
  8. Refridgerate the tea.

Cook's Notes

If you do not have large tea strainer, you can also use a sieve to filter out the barley when you pour it into a separate jug.
