Kimchi Jeon - Kimchi Pancake



The original recipe can be found on AllRecipes.

Prep time: ~15 min

Cook time: ~15 min

Servings: 4 (8 pancakes)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red kimchi, chopped and drained
  • 1/2 cup kimchi juice
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Korean red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds

Cooking Instructions

  1. To make the batter, stir the kimchi, kimchi juice, flour, eggs, and green onion in a bowl.
  2. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Using 1/4 cup of batter, pour into the skillet and spread as thinly as possible.
  4. Cook the pancake until set and lightly browned. Flip the pancake once and cook 3-5 minutes for each side.
  5. Make the dipping sauce by whisking together rice vinegar, sesame oil, soy sauce, red pepper flakes, and toasted sesame seeds. Serve with the kimchi pancakes.

Cook's Notes

This can be a moderately spicy dish, so adjust the amount of red pepper used in the recipe according to your preference.
