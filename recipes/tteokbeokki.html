Tteokbeokki - Korean Spicy Rice Cakes



The original recipe can be found on AllRecipes.

Prep time: ~5 min

Cook time: ~25 min

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 3 dried anchovies (large)
  • 3 tablespoons red pepper paste (gochujang)
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon corn syrup
  • 2 Korean fish cakes, sliced
  • 1/2 white onion, sliced
  • 1 green onion, sliced

Cooking Instructions

  1. Combine 3 cups of water and the 2 dried anchovies in a saucepan. (More anchovies can be added for a stronger taste).
  2. Bring to a boil and cook for 10 minutes. This makes the anchovy sauce base.
  3. Remove and discard the anchovies.
  4. Set the anchove sauce base aside to cool.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine red pepper paste, sugar, soy sauce, and corn syrup. Mix well.
  6. Add (defrosted) rice cakes, slice white onion, and mixture to the anchovy water.
  7. Bring the new mixture to a boil and cook for 5 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  8. Add spring onion and boil for 2 more minutes.
  9. Remove from heat. Stir well before serving hot.

Cook's Notes

  • This is a spicy dish, so adjust the amount of red pepper paste used in the recipe according to your preference.
  • Frozen rice cakes can be used for this recipe, but they must be defrosted before they are added to the mix. To defrost, microwave them in a bowl for 30 seconds.
  • The rice cakes should be chewy, with a little bit a spring. If they are boiled for too long, they will get mushy.
